Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden charges?
No hidden charges. The pricing you see is the one-time development fee. Note that for the Starter plan, Domain and Hosting are extra (₹800). After the first year, standard domain/hosting renewal fees apply.
How long does it take to create the website?
For Starter and Business plans, we usually deliver within 5-7 working days once we have all your business details and images. Premium plans may take up to 14 days.
Do I need to write the content myself?
If you have specific text, that's great! If not, just give us a brief about your business, and our team will write professional, SEO-friendly content for you.
Can I update the website later?
Yes! We build on platforms like WordPress, making it easy for you to edit text or images. We also offer affordable monthly maintenance packages if you want us to handle updates.
